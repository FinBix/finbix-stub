Four-Side Issuing Reconciliation

Issuing Reconciliation involves four positions:

  • Clearing (Financial) – member to member
  • Settlement – subset of member to member Clearing (excluding non-settled clearing such as bilateral agreements/H2H) + MC to member (fees)
  • Funds Transfer (MC debit invoice/SWIFT)
  • Card Balance GL portion as per external funds GL accounts (MC receivables/dues)

Payout Orchestration

As a Payout platform, FinBix receives payment instructions from Acquirer Banks and Payment Gateways, enriches the data according to Payee and Beneficiary Bank Details stored in FinBix system, generates Payment Instructions respective to Payout methods such as RTGS, Swift, ACH - and sends these payment instructions to settlement banks.
